About the Davenport PLA

The Davenport Provincial Liberal Association is a volunteerâ€‘driven organization dedicated to supporting Liberal values and engaging our community.

From Wallace Emerson, Dovercourt-Wallace Emerson Junction, Corso Italia, and Davenport Village to Little Portugal and the Junction Triangle, Davenport is a diverse and vibrant riding with deep community roots. The Davenport Provincial Liberal Association (DPLA) is a group of engaged residents committed to supporting community initiatives and participating in grassroots activities that promote Liberal values in our riding. We provide a voice for Davenport by regularly voicing our community's priorities to the Ontario Liberal Party, highlighting the issues that matter most to people who live and work in Davenport. Above all, we work to elect a Liberal MPP in Davenport through community outreach, canvassing, fundraising, and campaign activities. We welcome new members, volunteers, and supporters who want to help strengthen our riding and build a better future for our community.
